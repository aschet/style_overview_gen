# PREFIX

Composition: A waist-up portrait of a 40-year-old European woman with fair skin and long dark hair falling over her shoulders, wearing a plain T-shirt with a visible round neckline. She sits at a plain table, her body and face turned slightly to the left, her gaze directed to the left. She is drinking from a glass of beer, the rim resting against her lower lip, one arm resting on the table. Her expression is calm and relaxed. She is centered in the frame, eye-level view.
